Our Platform

An end-to-end, clinical-grade sleep diagnostic ecosystem

Synaptix combines a wearable ECG+EEG sensing hub, an intelligent mobile app, and cloud-based time-series AI to deliver clinical accuracy from the comfort of home.

What We're Building

Most home sleep tests capture airflow only. Our dual-biosignal approach captures both cardiac and brain activity — dramatically improving diagnostic completeness. Our proprietary deep learning architecture is built specifically for physiological time-series, not a wrapper around generic models.

Signal preprocessing is optimized at the hardware layer in co-design with the AI model architecture. This yields better accuracy and lower cloud inference cost — enabling 48-hour turnaround at a price point compatible with SaaS economics.

Every study processed improves our models. As our network of partner labs grows, model accuracy compounds — creating a data flywheel moat that widens over time against new entrants.
